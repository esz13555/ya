自Public transportations and taxis are available at the airport……….……

Bus
Get off at Kuandu MRT station Stop: Routes No. 223, S23, 838, R13, R22, and DanHai Line, get transfer Route R35, R55.
Get off at Kuandu Elementary School Stop: Routes No. 223, 302, 550, S23, 308, 682, 821 and 864.
MRT Commuters
Get off at Kuandu Station, bus routes No. R35, R55 and the university shuttle are available at exit 1. Please get off at the TNUA University Library stop.
